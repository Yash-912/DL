Employee Handbook - Leave Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Vacation Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year.
Vacation days do not roll over to the next calendar year.

2. Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

3. Maternity/Paternity Leave
The company provides 16 weeks of fully paid maternity leave and 8 weeks of fully paid paternity leave for the birth or adoption of a child.

4. Bereavement Leave
Employees may take up to 5 days of paid bereavement leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member.
